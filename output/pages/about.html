About

I am enthusiatic about python, data science, and art. I have a Phd in mathematics from the University of Illinois at Chicago. I really enjoy constructing demonstrations of techniques from data science, constructing visualizations, and turning them into art. I enjoy writing informational articles about statistics and textile art. I've written an undergraduate text book on virtual knot theory, An invitation to knot theory. I enjoy all forms of textile art in my free time.
